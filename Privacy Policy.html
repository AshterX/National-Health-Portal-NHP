Welcome to NATIONAL HEALTH PORT (Nheap.in). This Data and Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, protect, and disclose your personal information while using our services.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Services: We use your information to provide, improve, and customize our services, including managing health records and facilitating communication with healthcare providers.

By using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this Data and Privacy Policy.
